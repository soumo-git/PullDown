# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a professional, respectful, and inclusive community for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in communication.
- Focus critiques on code and decisions, not people.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions when unsure.
- Share actionable feedback with technical context.
- Respect maintainers' decisions on scope, quality, and release readiness.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments.
- Public or private intimidation.
- Deliberate disruption of discussions, reviews, or project workflow.
- Sharing private/confidential information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for interpreting and enforcing this Code of Conduct.
They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, or PRs that violate these standards.

Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent bans from project participation.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to the maintainers through the repository contact channels.
Provide links, timestamps, and any relevant context so the issue can be reviewed fairly.
